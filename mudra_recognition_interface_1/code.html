Pataka

"Flag"

This mudra is used to represent various concepts such as a forest, river, clouds, night, or the act of beginning a dance. The fingers are held straight and together, with the thumb bent to touch the base of the index finger.

Symbolism & Usage

  • self_improvement Denotes blessings, forbidding things, silence, and prowess.
  • waving_hand Used in the beginning of a Natya (dance-drama).
  • water_drop Can represent clouds, a forest, or a river.
