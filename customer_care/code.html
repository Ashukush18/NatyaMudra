Frequently Asked Questions

NatyaMudra is a mobile and web application designed to identify and analyze mudras from six traditional Indian dance forms. It serves as a digital guide for dancers, students, and enthusiasts.

Our app uses advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms. Simply use your device's camera to capture a mudra, and the app will identify it, providing details about its name, meaning, and usage in different dance forms.

NatyaMudra currently supports mudras from six major Indian classical dance forms: Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Odissi, Kuchipudi, Mohiniyattam, and Manipuri.

NatyaMudra offers a free version with access to a limited set of mudras. A premium subscription is available to unlock the full database of mudras, advanced analysis features, and tutorials.
