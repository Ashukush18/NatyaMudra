The Soul of Dance, Digitized.

NatyaMudra bridges the ancient art of Indian classical dance with modern technology, making the rich heritage of mudras accessible to everyone.

Cultural Significance of Mudras

Mudras are the unspoken language of Indian classical dance. These symbolic hand gestures convey complex emotions, narratives, and spiritual ideas, forming the expressive core of performances.

Our app honors this tradition by focusing on mudras from six major dance forms: Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, Odissi, Manipuri, Mohiniyattam, and Kathak, each with its unique gestural vocabulary.

The Technology Behind NatyaMudra

At its core, NatyaMudra is powered by a sophisticated machine learning model. We've trained it on a vast and diverse dataset of mudra images to ensure high accuracy in recognition.

The app provides real-time feedback through an intuitive interface, allowing you to capture a gesture and instantly learn about its name, meaning, and cultural context. We are constantly refining our algorithms to expand our database and improve the user experience.
